Our Peaceful Environment

At Gaya Massage, located in the vibrant Sunday walking street area of Chiang Mai, every detail has been designed to create a peaceful retreat from the bustling market atmosphere. Our space combines traditional Thai elements with modern comfort to provide authentic massage therapy in a relaxing environment.

From the moment you enter our location on Prapokklao Road, you'll find a tranquil space dedicated to traditional Thai massage and wellness. Our treatment areas feature comfortable settings designed for authentic Thai massage sessions.

We focus on providing genuine Thai massage therapy that promotes health and relaxation, right in the heart of Chiang Mai's popular Sunday market district.
